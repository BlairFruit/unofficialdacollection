### Major Features
* **Discord Rich Presence:** Discord will automatically show when you are playing the app, what page/act you are currently reading, and if you are snooping around in the menus.
* **Bulletproof Auto-Updater:** Rebuilt the auto-updater from the ground up. It now updates shit better, it won't corrupt your game files. It also displays a real-time `[OFFLINE MODE]` badge if you have no internet.

### Quality of Life
* **Window Memory:** It will now save your window size and monitor position across sessions.
* **Chapter/Scene Index:** Added a dropdown menu to the bottom navigation bar so you can instantly jump to specific Acts without guessing the page number. 
* **Content Safeguards:** If an image or video fails to download, the collection will now let you know if the file is called for.

### Extras & Content
* **Organized Gallery:** The Asset Gallery is now split into "ACT 1" and "ACT 2" tabs to make it easier to navigate. 
* **Spoiler Locks:** ACT 2 of the gallery (and the chapter index) is completely locked-out until you actually read past Act 1.

### Bug Fixes
* Squashed a bug where achievement popups would get cut off if you clicked to the next page too fast. They will now queue up and play on the next page.
